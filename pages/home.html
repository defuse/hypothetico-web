What is Hypothetico?

Hypothetico is a new quarterly electronic magazine. Hypothetico features short ASCII texts that make you think. We accept writings on any topic (fiction or non-fiction) and in any format, within the limitations of 7-bit ASCII.

What sets Hypothetico apart from other e-zines is our lack of regard for the truth. An interesting idea in its raw form, not constrained by the requirement of truth or rationality, can lead to new insights in existing fields, and even the creation of completely new fields of study. Of course, once the new insights are made, they should be subjected to reason and scientific rigor if they are to be used in practice. Hypothetico acts as a "safe zone" for the early publication strange, even controversial, ideas.

We are particularly interested in:

  • Isomorphisms between fields.
  • Strange ways of thinking of well-known things.
  • Strange ways of explaining things.
  • Multiple levels of meaning.
  • Anything thought-provoking.
