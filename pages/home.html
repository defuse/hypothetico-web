What is Hypothetico?

Hypothetico is a new quarterly electronic magazine. Hypothetico features short ASCII texts that make you think. We accept writings on any topic (fiction or non-fiction) and in any format, within the limitations of 7-bit ASCII.

Hypothetico is all about strange and controversial ideas. We do not require submissions to undergo any kind of peer review process. We do not even require submissions to contain a truth or to be rational. Hypothetico is a "safe zone" for the publication of interesting and thought provoking, but unusual, ideas. Readers must keep these facts in mind as they read. For more information about Hypothetico's philosophy, read the submissions page.

We are particularly interested in:

  • Isomorphisms between fields.
  • Strange ways of thinking of well-known things.
  • Strange ways of explaining things.
  • Multiple levels of meaning.
  • Anything thought-provoking.
